Our
Purpose

We simply can not stay silent anymore...

In Iran, discrimination against women and gender inequality have been “ recognized by the law “ for many years.

Iranian women's rights are severely curtailed under the Islamic Penal Code, which is a form of gender discrimination. Women in the Islamic Republic are treated unfairly in a number of areas, including marriage, divorce, child custody, inheritance, and the requirement to wear a headscarf starting at puberty.

The tragic death of the 22 year old Mahsa Amini, who passed away while in police custody after being detained by Iran’s “ morality police “for failing to properly cover her hair, has triggered a nation wide protest. Hundreds of protestors including children has been slain by the Iranian authorities.

We Are Liberty For Iran

We are a non-profit organisation who aim’s to spread awareness of the injustice that is happening in Iran and the revolution that Iranians in Iran and across the globe has started.

Using our voice as our weapon, we want people who are unaware of the situation to be aware and informed.

We believe that one day the women and the people of Iran will be free from injustice and discrimination by the regime that has been terrorizing the beautiful nation for decades.

The Founder

We Are Liberty For Iran was founded by a 23 year old Iranian-Danish Melisa Pour. In solidarity with the brave Iranian - Danish community they are on a mission to give voice to the women and people of Iran.
